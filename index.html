fish is watching

Modular performance collective exploring listening as method and process as form.

Bandcamp YouTube

1.
We do not play music, we enter the sound that already
unfolds and let the situation shape the form.

2.
The process matters more than the product.
Nothing repeats, nothing is polished to perfection.

3.
The collective is modular: each participant is a unique
module, that means a method, an experience, a sensitivity.

We do not perform, we let listening happen.
