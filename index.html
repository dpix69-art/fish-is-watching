1.
We do not play music, we enter the sound that already unfolds and let the situation shape the form.

2.
The process matters more than the product. Nothing repeats, nothing is polished to perfection.

3.
The collective is modular: each participant is a unique module, that means a method, an experience, a sensitivity.

4.
We do not perform, we let listening happen.
