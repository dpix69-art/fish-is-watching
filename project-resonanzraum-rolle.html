Resonanzraum Rolle

Stuttgart, 16.11.2025

Live performance and recording at the house of Günter Rolle.

The session takes place in the space where Günter Rolle once built and played his instruments. The house has its own tone and quiet history. Light, walls, and furniture hold the memory of earlier rehearsals.

We work with this memory as a resonant body. Microphones are placed close to objects and corners, contact mics follow surfaces, and small speakers move sound through the rooms. We listen to how air, wood, and instruments react to each other and let the space decide when the piece begins and ends.
